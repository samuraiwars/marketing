ABOUT THE GAME

You only need to own at least 1 Samurai Wars character NFT to start playing the game. The game will launch on Binance Smart Chain network and contrary to the usual standard, we strive to create not only a play to earn but a play and earn NFT game where everyone can enjoy the game.

The Land of Rising Sun is a nation where creatures and people from different tribes live together. They used to coexist in harmony until an unfortunate incident divided the land into multiple warring sides, each fighting for supremacy and hoping to be the ruler of all.

Today, the four greatest factions have risen above the rest and continue to battle one another in an endless fight for the throne. Samurai, Shinobi, Onmyoji, and Tengu are the best among their own people. Each has his own specialized expertise, making them equally formidable in the battlefield. More importantly, each is committed to sacrifice everything for the sake of attaining glory.

GAME MODE

Collect Unique NFT

Summon your NFT characters, complete side quests, do boss raid and earn a chance to get unique and powerful NFT equipments

Mission / Job

Assign your characters to do their mission to earn Ryo in-game currency which can be converted to BUDOU token. The stronger your characters are, the more Ryo you will earn.

Boss Raid

Weekly boss raid, get awarded NFT equpiments for being on the leaderboard based on the total damage. The NFT equipment will be in extremely limited amount and award high powerups to gain more Ryo

Clan Wars

Team up with other players, contribute Ryo, advance the clan and get more benefit during boss raid and battle for clan supremacy

Samurai

Samurai was born to a long line of aristocratic warriors, spending his life fighting battles for the lords and defending their honour. His entire family were perceived by civilians with both awe and fear, which served them all well as they pride themselves for their stoic manners and unwavering loyalty. He is a figure of calm and quiet, yet lethal.

Like the rest of his faction, Samurai is skilled in the battlefield and court alike, weaving through the intricacies of politics with as much finesse as he wields his swords. When societal balance in the Land of Rising Sun is threatened, so is their place of honour and privilege. It is only natural for him to fight for his people and retain their prominence.

When in battle, Samurai is a fearless soldier who shows his foes no mercy. His people are the only ones allowed to wear two swords and this skill translates well on the combat zone. Deliberate and strong, he dons an imposing armour that intimidates the enemy, while his own movements are inspired by a mix of various martial arts.

Samurai provide 2.5% buff on defense and 2.5% buff on offense.

Shinobi

Shinobi is the definition of a silent assassin. Trained since youth to be a ruthless mercenary and discreet spy, she thrives in obscurity. She comes out from the hidden nooks of the city once the sun sets, tiptoeing under shade of darkness and working in utter silence. Only at daybreak will word of her attacks or sabotages come out, and by then Shinobi will have vanished like wisps of night.

They whisper her name under silent breaths and talk of her notoriety in hushed tones. Yet nobody knows what he looks like or who he really is. Feared as he is, Shinobi grows unsatisfied with this shroud of mystery. So, when chaos ensues in the Land of Rising Sun, he knows this is her chance to rise from the shadows.

She’s extremely stealthy and quick that her enemies often realize they have been targeted too late. A master of disguise and staying invisible, Shinobi hides her weapons in loose clothing and unassuming garb.

Shinobi provide 5% buff on defense.

Onmyoji

Onmyoji is versed in the ancient arts of natural science and divination, an old discipline which blurs the line between physics and magic, between mortals and the gods. He comes from a family that has always challenged the restraints of humanity and shown time and again that humans are capable of boundless actions.

His power and ability to touch the realms beyond should grant him authority over all that lives. Instead, he is bound by some ridiculous mortal law. After centuries serving under the imperial court, helping rulers make the right decisions and achieve the impossible, Onmyoji decides he has had enough.

The Land of Rising Sun has fallen into disarray and Onmyoji will seize this opportunity to show the world the true power of his people. He does not need much to win a battle: just a few hand gestures and a clear mind, and he can manipulate the elements of nature to bring even the toughest gladiator to his knees.

Onmyoji provide 5% buff on offense.
